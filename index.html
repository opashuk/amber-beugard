Capturing and highlighting a woman’s journey
of what self love means to them and sharing
their stories through art.

The “Rediscover: A Journey of Self-love” campaign
is my way of highlighting every woman’s journey
in discovering their strength and the ability
to overcome obstacles gracefully.

It is a revelation of how self-love is born in the life
of women today, and it can only be made visible
and meaningful when these women speak out
and raise their voices.

There was a time I became a shadow of myself,
and because of the hurt in me, I found it hard
to enjoy life and see how wonderful it could be.
At that time, I enclosed myself into a world,
so void, all that surrounded me was frustration
and depression.

The “Rediscover: A Journey of Self-love”

The campaign is that safe space where women
of diverse cultures share their stories in the most
beautiful and creative way with imagery and videos—
just letting their hair down with great makeup
and spending a day out laughing and having fun
around other like-minded women.

For me, photography is about using my gift
and passion for bringing people together;
to capture, create and share special moments
in time. Supporting and sponsoring this campaign
isn’t about making money but creating a special
place in my city where more women can feel free
to express themselves creatively by sharing
their trials and triumphs.

This is the goal of Kayleen Imagery. A mission
to bring a community of women together
through art, poetry, storytelling, and imagery.
